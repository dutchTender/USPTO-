USPTO Trademarks office is working to replace the form based filing system with intutitive widgets and guided wizard based filing workflows. This initiative is designed to simplify the Trademark filing and respondant process, and aims to greatly reduce complexity and deliver a superior seemless user experience while maintain the highest level of industry standards in security.

The new uspto.gov accounts are designed for individuals, not groups or organizations. Future updates will add the ability for organizations to share information between colleagues.
